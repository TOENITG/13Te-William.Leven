Goal

Emycia is a community meant to go across games and platforms, we want to be the type of community you can be a part of no matter if you play skyrim on xbox or terraria on pc. That way we hope to bind a strong web of contacts that will keep the community alive for many years.

How it started

To begin with everything started with minecraft, I myself started playing minecraft and after a few months of playing I were given a server which I later shut down because I wanted something i started on my own instead of the endless work with building on something someone else started. But while working on the new server the idea of Emycia came across my mine and I taled to some old contacts about it and all of them were willing to help, i also talked with some former team orion members and we decided to rebuild T.O. from the staff of Emycia. After that i stared the real work with the server vto make a stable pilar for both Emycia and T.O. to rely on.
